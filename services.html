Keri provides in-home physical therapy services to children in the greater Portland-metro area. She works with children of all ages, from birth through the teenage years, and all diagnoses, including: developmental delay, torticollis, plagiocephaly, toe walking, coordination disorders, and neuro-developmental diagnoses, such as: Cerebral Palsy, genetic syndromes, hydrocephalus, brain injury, Autism Spectrum Disorders, Spina Bifida, etc.
