Keri Nelson is a state-licensed physical therapist (PT). She is a Board-Certified Clinical Specialist (PCS) in pediatric physical therapy with over ten years of experience working with children. Keri received her Bachelor of Arts degree at Colorado College in 2007 and her Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) at the University of Colorado in 2013. Keri has experience working with children in their homes and schools and both the outpatient and inpatient settings. Keri has previously worked at Doernbecher Children’s Hospital and Children’s Intensive Therapy.

Keri is a mom of two young children. She enjoys spending time with her family and two cats. Keri loves running, hiking, and exploring the beautiful Pacific Northwest. She loves to read and is learning to cook more toddler approved meals.
