Keri runs a home-based pediatric physical therapy business in the greater Portland, OR area. She enjoys working with children and their families in their natural environments where they are most comfortable, including their homes and community spaces. Keri believes that all children have the potential to make progress and loves supporting children and their families to optimize functional mobility and participation in all of life’s activities. Keri is passionate about working with children, especially children with developmental disabilities.
